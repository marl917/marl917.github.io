Marlene Careil

I am a final-year PhD student at FAIR Paris and Telecom Paris (LTCI team). I am advised by Jakob Verbeek and Stephane Lathuiliere. My research focuses on improving controllability and diversity of deep generative models (GANs and diffusion models), as well as few-shot image generation. I also explore perceptual compression which leverages generative models to improve realism of reconstructed images.

I received a MSc/BSc in Applied Mathematics & Computer Science from CentraleSupélec (diplôme d'Ingénieur) and a MSc in Mathematical Sciences from Oxford University.
